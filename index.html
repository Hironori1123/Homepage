Hiro(nori) Katsuda

Postdoctoral Fellow, University of Toronto Scarborough, Department of Language Studies

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto Scarborough, working under the mentorship of Professor Yoonjung Kang. Before this role, I earned my Ph.D. in Linguistics from the University of California, Los Angeles. My research interests lie in computational and experimental approaches to phonology, experimental phonetics, and psycholinguistics. I employ a diverse range of research methodologies, including corpus analysis, computational modeling, and experiments. You can find my CV here.
