Hiro(nori) Katsuda

PhD candidate, UCLA Department of Linguistics

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Toronto Scarborough, where I work under the guidance of Professor Yoonjung Kang. My research focuses mainly on theoretical and experimental phonology, phonetics, as well as fieldwork. I completed my PhD studies at the University of California, Los Angeles. My dissertation examined the multiple factors that influence the assignment of loanword accents in Japanese. My research employed a multi-faceted approach, including corpus analysis, computational modeling and experiments.
