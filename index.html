Hiro(nori) Katsuda

Postdoctoral Fellow, University of Toronto Scarborough, Department of Language Studies

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto Scarborough, under the mentorship of Professor Yoonjung Kang. Prior to this role, I earned my Ph.D. from the Linguistics Department at the University of California, Los Angeles. My research interests lie in theoretical, experimental, and compulational phonology, phonetics, and fieldwork. I employ a diverse set of researh methodologies, such as corpus analysis, computational modeling, and experimental studies, intonational analysis, and fieldwork.
