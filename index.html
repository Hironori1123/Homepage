Hiro(nori) Katsuda

Visiting Assistant Professor
University of Kansas, Department of Linguistics

I am a visiting assistant professor at the University of Kansas. Prior to this position, I served as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto Scarborough, where I worked under the mentorship of Professor Yoonjung Kang. I earned my Ph.D. in Linguistics from the University of California, Los Angeles. My research interests lie in computational and experimental approaches to phonology, experimental phonetics, and psycholinguistics. I employ a diverse range of research methodologies, including corpus analysis, computational modeling, and experiments. You can find my CV here.
